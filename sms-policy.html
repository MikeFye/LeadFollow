SMS Messaging Policy

LeadFollow enables businesses to communicate with customers via SMS when responding to missed phone calls or customer inquiries.

Consent

Customers provide consent to receive SMS messages by initiating contact with the business, such as calling the business phone number. If the call is missed, a follow‑up text message may be sent to continue the conversation regarding the customer's inquiry.

Message Purpose

Messages are used for purposes including:

  • Responding to missed calls
  • Customer service communication
  • Appointment scheduling

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on the customer's interaction with the business.

Opt‑Out Instructions

Customers may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP.

After opting out, no additional SMS messages will be sent unless the customer initiates a new conversation.

For assistance, reply HELP.

Standard message and data rates may apply.
