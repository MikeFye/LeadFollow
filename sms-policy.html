SMS Messaging Policy

LeadFollow enables businesses to communicate with customers via SMS when responding to missed phone calls or customer inquiries. LeadFollow may also communicate directly with users who contact LeadFollow for demo, support, or product inquiries.

Consent

Customers provide consent to receive SMS messages by initiating contact with a business or LeadFollow using a phone number powered by LeadFollow.

If a customer places a call and the call is not answered, a follow-up text message may be sent to continue the conversation regarding the customer’s inquiry.

By initiating contact, the user agrees to receive SMS messages related to their request.

Message Purpose

Messages sent through LeadFollow are used for purposes including:

  • Responding to missed calls
  • Customer service communication
  • Appointment scheduling and follow-ups

LeadFollow sends messages on behalf of the business the customer contacted or directly in response to user inquiries.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on the customer’s interaction and responses.

Opt-Out Instructions

Customers may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP.

After opting out, no additional SMS messages will be sent unless the customer initiates a new conversation.

For assistance, reply HELP.

Fees

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on the customer’s mobile carrier.

Privacy

LeadFollow does not use SMS communications for marketing purposes and does not sell or share personal information with third parties.

For more information, please review our Privacy Policy.
