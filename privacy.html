Privacy Policy

LeadFollow respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is collected and used when individuals interact with businesses that use the LeadFollow platform, as well as when users contact LeadFollow directly.

Information We Collect

When a customer contacts a business using a phone number connected to LeadFollow, or contacts LeadFollow directly, we may collect basic information including:

  • Phone number
  • Message content
  • Interaction details such as missed calls and responses

This information is collected solely to facilitate communication between the customer and the business or to respond to user inquiries.

How Information Is Used

Information collected through LeadFollow is used only for:

  • Responding to customer inquiries
  • Facilitating appointment scheduling
  • Supporting ongoing customer communication

LeadFollow acts as a communication platform on behalf of the business. Information may be accessible to the business using LeadFollow to manage and respond to customer interactions.

LeadFollow does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

SMS Communications

When a customer initiates contact with a business or LeadFollow, for example by calling a phone number, and the call is not answered, LeadFollow may send a follow-up SMS message to continue the conversation.

By initiating contact, the user is consenting to receive SMS messages related to their inquiry.

  • Message frequency varies depending on the interaction
  • Message and data rates may apply

Customers may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP.

Data Security

LeadFollow takes reasonable measures to protect customer information and ensure it is used only for its intended purpose.
