Privacy Policy

LeadFollow respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is collected and used when customers interact with businesses using the LeadFollow platform.

Information We Collect

When customers contact a business using a phone number connected to LeadFollow, basic information such as phone numbers and message content may be collected in order to facilitate communication between the customer and the business.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only for purposes related to customer service communication, responding to inquiries, and appointment scheduling.

LeadFollow does not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

SMS Communications

Customers may receive SMS messages when they contact a business that uses LeadFollow. Message frequency varies depending on the conversation.

Standard message and data rates may apply.

Customers may opt out of SMS communication at any time by replying STOP.

For assistance, reply HELP.
